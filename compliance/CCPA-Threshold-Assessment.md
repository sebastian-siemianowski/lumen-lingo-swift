## 1. CCPA Applicability Thresholds

The CCPA (Cal. Civ. Code § 1798.140(d)) applies to a for-profit business that collects California residents' personal information AND meets **any** of these thresholds:

| Threshold | Status | Notes |
|-----------|--------|-------|
| Annual gross revenue > $25 million | **NOT MET** | LumenShore is pre-revenue / early-stage |
| Buys, sells, or shares personal information of ≥ 100,000 California consumers/households annually | **NOT MET** | Minimal data collection; no selling or sharing |
| Derives ≥ 50% of annual revenue from selling or sharing consumers' personal information | **NOT MET** | LumenShore does not sell or share personal information |

## 2. Assessment Conclusion

**LumenLingo does NOT currently meet CCPA thresholds.**

However, we have chosen to **voluntarily comply** with CCPA/CPRA requirements because:

1. **Growth preparedness** — as the user base grows, thresholds may be met in the future
2. **Best practice** — CCPA compliance aligns with our existing GDPR obligations
3. **Trust** — demonstrating compliance builds trust with US users
4. **Multi-state alignment** — Virginia (VCDPA), Colorado (CPA), Connecticut (CTDPA), and other state laws have similar requirements without revenue thresholds in some cases
